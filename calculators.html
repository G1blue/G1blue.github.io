Pick a Calculator!

General Calculator

The general calculator offers the operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication. Additional information can be found on the home page.

Many Addends Calculator

The Many Addends Calculator offers the sum of a range of numbers. For example, if you write 1 as the lowest number and 5 as the highest, it will calculate 1+2+3+4+5. Additional information can be found on the home page.

Radicals

The Radical Calculator, or simply Radicals, is taking a root of a number. For example, if the root is 2 and the number is 4, then the output will be sqrt(4) or 2. Additional information can be found on the home page.

Exponents

The Exponent Calculator, or simply Exponents, is taking a power of a number. For example, if the number is 2 and the exponent is 2, the output will be 2^2 or 4. Additional information can be found on the home page.
